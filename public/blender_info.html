Professional 3D Creation

Everything you need to bring your creative vision to life.

Modeling

Powerful modeling tools with polygonal modeling, sculpting, UV unwrapping, and more. Create anything from hard surface models to organic characters.

Sculpting & Painting

Industry-leading sculpting tools with dynamic topology, multi-resolution support, and powerful texture painting capabilities.

Animation & Rigging

Full-featured animation system with automatic skinning, pose library, and motion tracking. Bring your characters to life.

Rendering

Production-ready rendering with Cycles and Eevee engines. Real-time viewport preview and photorealistic final renders.

Simulation

Realistic physics simulations including fluids, smoke, cloth, particles, and rigid body dynamics.

Video Editing

Built-in video sequence editor for basic to advanced editing tasks. Perfect for creating complete productions.
